What parents need to know

Your child’s brain continues to develop until their early 20s – which means it’s a precious thing to protect. Some parents give their kids alcohol with the best intentions, thinking it will control the amount they consume. But the evidence shows that if alcohol is easy to get, people drink more. When parents provide alcohol to their children they make it very easy for young people to access it.

Your child and alcohol. Think again now.

‘Think again now’ is an awareness campaign developed by the National Social Marketing Centre. The National Social Marketing Centre is a not-for-profit Community Interest Company based in the UK. Funding for the campaign was received from Hastings and Rother Clinical Commissioning Group.
